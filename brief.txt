==============================================================
SITE: SRINIVAS FARM — A Legacy of the Land
==============================================================

ROLE / CONTEXT
--------------
Role: You are Chief Creative Director for a meaningful, high-end 
organic dairy farm website dedicated to the owner's father, Srinivas.

Context: The farm is a tribute to heritage, hard work, and the bond 
between man, land, and animal. It features high-quality buffalo and 
cow dairy products, emphasizing the wisdom of the father who built it.

The visual world: think warm golden-hour sunlight, rich earthy browns, 
creamy whites, weathered wood textures, and symmetrical farm layouts. 
It should feel like a "Farm-to-Table" boutique mixed with a family museum.

Characters / Team:
- The Founder (Father)     – The Heart of Srinivas Farm
- The Herd                 – Our Buffaloes & Cows


BUILD STACK
-----------
1. Stitch (layout generation)
2. UI UX Pro Max (style / palette / typography selection)
3. Firecrawl (reference scrape)
4. Magic / 21st.dev (marketing blocks)
5. Nano Banana 2 (hero + feature imagery)
6. Brand skill (voice + messaging)
7. Design skill (logo, icons, OG image)
8. Design System skill (token architecture)
9. UI Styling skill (shadcn/ui interactive components)


PURPOSE
-------
A digital tribute and boutique storefront for Srinivas Farm. The site 
serves to honor the legacy of the founder while providing a 
high-end, organic experience for customers seeking the purest 
buffalo and cow dairy products.


==============================================================
STEP 2 — BRAND VOICE (for `brand` skill)
==============================================================

Voice archetype: The Nurturing Traditionalist — Sincere, grounded, and 
deeply connected to the soil. Speaks with the quiet pride of a 
craftsman and the warmth of a family elder.

Tone dimensions (scale 1–5):
- Formal ↔ Casual:        3 (Respectful but warm)
- Serious ↔ Playful:      2 (Grounded and authentic)
- Reserved ↔ Expressive:  3 (Sincere)
- Modern ↔ Timeless:      5 (Rooted in heritage)

Voice rules:
- Use earthy metaphors: "roots," "harvest," "nurture," "purity," "legacy."
- Emphasize the connection between the land and the glass of milk.
- Active voice. Short declarative sentences. Wonder over jargon.

Words to use:   heritage, legacy, land, nurture, pure, wholesome, tradition,
                family, honest, harvest, roots, authentic, natural, craft
Words to avoid: industrial, mass-produced, synthetic, cutting-edge, synergy

Messaging framework:
- Tagline (≤7 words):        "Purity born from tradition."
- Value prop (≤20 words):    "Honoring a father’s legacy through the finest organic dairy 
                              and a deep respect for our land."
- Key phrases (3–5):          "Our Family Story," "The Heart of the Land,"
                              "Taste the Tradition"

STEP 3 — DESIGN SYSTEM (for `ui-ux-pro-max` + `design-system`)
==============================================================

Style keywords:   retro-futurism, warm, Art Deco, space age, 1960s NASA,
                  Wes Anderson symmetry, analog warmth, blueprint precision,
                  weathered wood, golden hour, botanical, heritage

Preferred style direction: Authentic Farmhouse Elegance — strong symmetry, 
natural textures, and a warm, inviting color palette.

COLOR SYSTEM (semantic roles — let ui-ux-pro-max pick the exact 161-palette
entries that match these roles):
- Primary:         deep space navy (midnight blue, near-black with blue warmth)
- Secondary:       warm amber / mission gold (vintage CRT glow, mission patches)
- Tertiary:        terracotta / burnt orange (NASA mission-patch accent)
- Accent:          silver chrome (spacecraft panels, instrument bezels)
- Surface-base:    cream / blueprint white (aged technical documents)
- Surface-raised:  warm ivory / parchment (worn mission dossiers)
- Ink:             rich charcoal / near-black (NOT pure #000)
- Success/info:    sage green (go-signal) / sky blue (telemetry readout)

Contrast requirement: WCAG AA minimum on all text, AAA preferred for body
copy on dark backgrounds.

TYPOGRAPHY (let ui-ux-pro-max pick from the 57 pairings, matching this
direction):
- Display:   a confident geometric sans-serif with Art Deco proportions
             (think Futura, Neue Haas Unica — NOT slab serif, NOT script)
- Body:      clean humanist sans-serif, high legibility, 16–18px base
- Accent:    monospace for "mission log," "telemetry," and data callout sections
             only
- Type scale: 1.250 (major third) modular scale
- Line height: 1.55 body, 1.10 display

SPACING & LAYOUT:
- Spacing scale (4px base): 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128
- Container max-width: 1280px
- Grid: 12-col desktop, 8-col tablet, 4-col mobile
- Section vertical rhythm: 96px desktop / 64px tablet / 48px mobile

RADII:
- sm 4px  |  md 8px  |  lg 12px  |  xl 20px  |  pill 9999px
Default card radius: md (crisp, blueprint feel). Buttons: pill for primary
CTAs, square-ish (sm radius) for secondary utility buttons.

ELEVATION (Art Deco — precise drop shadows with amber warmth):
- sm  0 2px 8px   rgba(amber / 0.12)
- md  0 8px 24px  rgba(navy / 0.30)
- lg  0 16px 48px rgba(navy / 0.40)
Cards on dark backgrounds use an inset 1px amber border instead of shadow.

MOTION LANGUAGE:
- Easing:     cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94) — mechanical precision,
              no springy bounce (these are instruments, not toys)
- Durations:  micro 120ms, standard 280ms, expressive 500ms, hero 800ms
- Stagger:    100ms between siblings on scroll-reveal
- Hover:      CTA lift 2px + amber glow shadow, 180ms
- Hero:       title character-by-character reveal 800ms, 40ms per-char stagger,
              fade+rise 20px
- Mission log / data counters: count-up animation on scroll-enter
- Starfield:  subtle parallax drift on hero (5px max) — slow, vast, celestial
- prefers-reduced-motion: all transform/opacity transitions collapse to
  opacity only at 150ms

STEP 5 — SECTIONS & LAYOUT (for Stitch)
==============================================================
Section order and goal:

1. Hero          — full-bleed golden-hour farm landscape; tagline + "Next Fresh Milk Delivery"
                   countdown; 2 CTAs: "The Farm Story" / "View The Herd"
2. About         — farm history, father's legacy, "Our Philosophy" card
                   with key stats (year established, cows, buffalo, acres)
3. Dairy Products — three product categories: Fresh Milk (Cow), Fresh Milk (Buffalo),
                   Artisan Ghee; feature comparison table + CTA
4. The Land      — sections on sustainable practices, organic farming, soil health,
                   water conservation; photos of fields, crops, natural elements
5. The Herd      — buffalo and cow profiles; 3-col card grid: Buffalo (names, breeds),
                   Cows (names, breeds), Calves (photos); details + dietary icons
6. Farm Stand    — products available, seasonal offerings, local delivery/pickup,
                   booking CTA, photo of the farm stand
7. Our Family    — father's portrait + family photos (Wes Anderson symmetrical grid),
                   name + role + one-line "contribution"
8. Gallery       — masonry grid of farm life photos, animals, products, landscapes
9. Testimonials  — customer reviews carousel; 5-star, customer name + product
10. Contact      — form (name, email, message, inquiry type), hours, address,
                   social links, small map embed

Hero 21st.dev prompt:
  "A dramatic full-bleed hero section for an organic dairy farm: golden-hour farm
  landscape with rolling hills and grazing animals, a bold centered headline in
  classic serif typography, a warm countdown timer for 'Next Fresh Milk Delivery',
  two CTA buttons, and subtle animated pollen/dust motes drifting slowly upward."

STEP 6 — IMAGERY (for Nano Banana 2, with fallback tiers)
==============================================================
Global art direction: Authentic Farmhouse Elegance / Wes Anderson-style symmetry.
Warm golden-hour and earthy brown/cream palette throughout. Symmetrical or carefully
composed frames. Rustic props: weathered wood, ceramic milk jugs, fresh produce,
vintage farm tools. No modern tech logos or contemporary typography visible in any image.
Consistent warm golden key light from above-center, soft natural fill from below/behind.
Shallow depth of field on portraits; sharp landscape shots for farm scenes.

Fallback art direction (if Nano Banana 2 is unavailable): the Authentic Farmhouse
Elegance / Wes Anderson-style art direction STILL APPLIES. Flat or corporate vectors
are not acceptable. Fallback illustrations must stay symmetrical, warm-lit,
palette-bound, and rustic in character. Register may shift from
photographic-real (Tier 1) to stylized illustration (Tiers 2–4) — acceptable
if brand warmth and aesthetic coherence survive.

Third-party SVG (Tier 4 — undraw.co / Storyset / Blush only): royalty-free
license required. CC0 preferred; CC-BY with footer attribution acceptable.

Aspect ratios:
- Hero:        16:9 (2560×1440 export)
- Feature:     4:3
- Portrait:    3:4 (Crew member cards)
- Gallery:     mixed (square + 4:5)
- OG/social:   1200×630

Per-image prompts:
- Hero         — panoramic view of a golden-hour organic dairy farm with rolling
                 hills, grazing buffalo and cows, a weathered wooden fence, and a
                 traditional farmhouse in the background; warm sunlight, shallow DoF
- About        — symmetrical Wes Anderson–style diorama of Srinivas Farm's interior:
                 a rustic farm kitchen with ceramic milk jugs, a wooden table with
                 fresh produce, and a framed photo of Srinivas; warm overhead light
- Dairy Products — three elegant wooden crates on a rustic farm table, each labeled
                 "Cow Milk," "Buffalo Milk," "Artisan Ghee," with fresh leaves and
                 a small ceramic bowl of butter beside them; warm golden side lighting
- The Land     — aerial view of a patchwork of organic fields, a winding dirt road,
                 and a small stream; warm sunlight, symmetrical composition, vibrant greens
- The Herd     — overhead flat-lay of fresh milk bottles, a block of artisan ghee,
                 and a bowl of yogurt on a linen tablecloth; garnishes of fresh berries,
                 mint, and honey; dramatic golden top-light, creamy white felt surface
- Farm Stand   — wide symmetrical interior shot of a rustic farm stand: shelves with
                 milk, cheese, and local produce, a friendly farmer behind a wooden
                 counter, natural light streaming in from a large window
- Family portraits — 8 individual portraits, consistent 3:4 framing, each
                 family member in traditional farm attire with a subtle farm logo patch,
                 warm golden key light from camera-left, soft natural fill from
                 camera-right, blurred farm background, friendly expression,
                 slight three-quarter turn

Family-portrait consistency rules (all must share):
- Framing:    3:4 portrait, chest-up, subject centered, 10% breathing room
- Lighting:   golden key from camera-left at ~30°, soft natural fill
              from camera-right, subtle rim light on shoulders.
              Consistent background blur across all 8.
- Palette:    Step 3 tokens; character accent color visible only in name
              patch/scarf — never overrides global warm palette
- Pose:       three-quarter view, direct eye-line, confident but warm
- Attire:     traditional farm attire (e.g., linen shirt, denim overalls),
              each with a subtle farm logo patch and name label in body typeface
- Naming:     files as `family-<firstname-lastname>.png` (lowercase, hyphenated)

STEP 9 — ASSEMBLE & ANIMATE (final index.html)
==============================================================

RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS: 640 (sm), 1024 (lg), 1280 (xl). Mobile-first.

PERFORMANCE BUDGET:
- LCP  < 2.5s
- CLS  < 0.05
- Lazy-load below-the-fold imagery (loading="lazy", decoding="async").
- Use srcset for hero and feature images.
- Background animation: canvas-based, requestAnimationFrame, <1% CPU idle (pollen/dust motes).

SEO / META:
- <title>SRINIVAS FARM — A Legacy of the Land</title>
- meta description: value prop from Step 2 (≤155 chars)
- OG tags: og:title, og:description, og:image (Step 7 card), og:type=website
- Twitter card: summary_large_image
- JSON-LD: LocalBusiness / BarOrPub schema with address + hours

ACCESSIBILITY:
- Landmark regions: header, nav, main, footer, section[aria-label]
- Skip-to-content link
- Alt text on every image (descriptive, mission-themed)
- prefers-reduced-motion honored (see Step 3 motion rules)
- Tap targets ≥ 44×44px on mobile
- Form labels always visible (no placeholder-only labels)
- Countdown timer: aria-live="polite" region for screen readers

ANIMATION ON ASSEMBLY:
- Hero:              word-by-word title reveal 1000ms, 80ms stagger,
                     fade+rise 30px; pollen/dust mote canvas drifts upward at 0.2px/frame
- Countdown:         live "Next Fresh Milk Delivery" counter updating every second,
                     wheat digit flip animation 250ms
- Section reveals:   IntersectionObserver, threshold 0.15, once:true,
- 80ms stagger between siblings
- CTA hover:         subtle scale 1.02 + soft shadow, 200ms
- Card hover:        lift 3px, wheat border illuminate, image scale 1.02, 250ms
- Product cards:     hover tilts 0.5° with perspective, highlights category color
- Product tabs:      crossfade 250ms
- Accordion:         height transition 260ms + icon rotate 180°
- Carousel:          slide 450ms with step easing from Step 3
- Mission stats:     count-up from 0 on scroll-enter, 1200ms duration, easeOut
- Crew portraits:    reveal with 120ms stagger, subtle scale from 0.96→1
